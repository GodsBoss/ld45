Manual

Controls

Key What for?
A / D Turn left / right
W / S Move forward / backward
I Chop trees, mine rocks. You need a few hits for it to work. Stand like this, then press i a few times:

To mine rocks, you need pickaxes (see below).
J / L Choose special action. Standing in front of a crafting table or furnace gives you other options.
K Invoke special action, like smelting or crafting, planting a tree, etc.

Pickaxe tiers

There are three tiers of pickaxes, every tier can mine everything the previous tier can mine and more:

  1. The wooden pickaxe is entirely made of wood. It can mine normal rocks, but nothing else.
  2. After mining some rocks, you can craft the stone pickaxe. With it, you can mine iron ore and coal.
  3. Smelting iron ore in a furnace gives you iron, which can be crafted into an iron pickaxe. This is the best pickaxe, which can mine gold ore and diamonds.

Winning

You must craft the crown of epic nerdiness to win. Rumors say you need a few (3) diamonds and some (8) gold ingots.

Losing

Enemies were planned, but didn't make it into the game. You can die of hunger, but only very slooooowly.
